Distribution Strategy

Select how your annual portfolio withdrawal is calculated each year. Constant Dollar treats your expense inputs as fixed real withdrawals. Guardrail strategies dynamically adjust spending based on market performance.

Optional. If your distribution strategy calculates a withdrawal below this amount, this floor will be used instead. Represents your non-negotiable baseline spending in today’s dollars — the floor amount grows with inflation each year, so a $40,000 floor at retirement preserves today’s $40,000 of purchasing power. Leave blank or $0 for no floor.

A high minimum withdrawal relative to your portfolio may accelerate depletion. The simulation will deplete the portfolio rather than violate the minimum floor.
